Songbirds

The Floating Muses

Overview

Songbirds are a group of birds known for their vocal abilities.
They are known for their melodious songs and are often kept as pets for their beautiful tunes.
Songbirds are found all over the world and are known for their bright colors and cheerful songs.
They are a diverse group of birds that includes many different species, each with their unique appearances.
