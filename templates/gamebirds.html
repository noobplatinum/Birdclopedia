Gamebirds

Domesticated Avians

Overview

Gamebirds are birds that are raised in captivity for the purpose of hunting. They are known for their
delicious meat and are often served as a delicacy. Gamebirds are typically found in the wild, but
domesticated gamebirds are also common. They are known for their beautiful plumage and are often
kept as pets.
