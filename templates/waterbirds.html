Waterbirds

Wanderers of the Waters

Overview

Waterbirds are a diverse group of birds that are well-adapted to aquatic environments.
They can be found in a variety of habitats, including freshwater lakes, rivers, and marshes.
Waterbirds have a number of unique adaptations that allow them to thrive in these environments.
They are commonly equipped with webbed feet for swimming, long necks for foraging in the water, and waterproof feathers that help keep them dry.
