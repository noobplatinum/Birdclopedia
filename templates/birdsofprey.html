Birds of Prey

Predators of the Sky

Overview

Birds of prey are carnivorous birds that hunt and feed on other animals.
In terms of ferocity, they have little competition in the animal kingdom, let alone the avian world.
They are known for their keen eyesight, powerful talons, and sharp beaks.
They are also known for their speed and agility, which they use to catch their prey.
